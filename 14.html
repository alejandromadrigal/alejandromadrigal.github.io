    Internet protocols

  • -Millions of computers all over the world communicating with one another
  • -Protocols are simple language let computers share common language
  • -Internet uses the internet protocol suite
  • -TCP/IP created in 1973
  • -Application layer- HTTP, FTP, SMTP, DNS, RIP, SNMP
  • -LINK Layer - wireless LAN, Ethernet, ATM
  • -Transport layer - TCP, UDP
  • -Internet layer - IPv4, IPv6
  • -Internet would not work if it weren't for these protocols
